Frequently Asked Questions

How are Toll Taxes, State Entry Taxes, and Parking Fees handled?

Our quotes clearly specify whether Toll Taxes and State Entry Fees are included in the fare or are payable on actuals by the guest during the trip. There are absolutely no hidden markups on government tolls.

Are driver night allowances charged extra?

A driver night allowance of ₹250 is automatically added to the billing ONLY if the travel extends between 10:00 PM and 6:00 AM. This allowance goes directly to the driver for overnight rest and support.

What is the difference between One-Way Drop and Round-Trip billing?

One-Way Drop rates include a subsidized flat distance fee with no return trip driver charges. Round-trip rates require a minimum average driving limit of 250 km per calendar day, which includes driver overnight allowances and covers the vehicle's return distance.

Is luggage space limited inside the cabs?

Yes. Each vehicle category displays the exact passenger seat capacity and standard luggage bag limits (e.g., 2 bags for Sedan, 4 bags for SUV). If you have extra luggage, you can request an overhead carrier during the checkout flow.
